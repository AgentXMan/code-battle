PROBLEM STATEMENT

Organic and inorganic industrial pollutants and effluents released into rivers pollute the water. Paper, pulp, chemical, textile, and dyeing industries, petroleum refineries, tanneries, and electroplating industries are the main culprits in this regard, releasing dyes, detergents, acids, salts, and heavy metals like lead and mercury into water bodies, as well as pesticides, fertilizers, synthetic chemicals with carbon, plastics, and rubber.

Our solution

The plan is to help achieve the 3 SDGs using arduino along with pH sensors and temperature sensors. If the pH and temperature is optimal, the water can be discharged from the industry. This device can also be fitted into buoys (floating devices on water bodies). If the pH and temperature of the water body near the industry is outside the optimal range, authorities can be warned and the industry will be fined.
